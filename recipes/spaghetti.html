Chicken Spaghetti

Description

Delicious, easy to make spaghetti with chicken, mushrooms, and diced chile peppers.

Ingredients

  • 1 package (12 ounces) of angel hair pasta
  • 2 cups of chopped, cooked chickne breast
  • 1 can (10.75 ounces) of condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 1 can (10 ounces) of diced tomatoes with green chile peppers
  • 3/4 package of cubed, processed cheese
  • 1/2 jar of sliced mushrooms, drained
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of lightly salted water to boil. Cook the angel hair pasta in the boiling water, occasionally stirring, until the pasta is tender yet firm to the bite. This should take around 4 to 5 minutes. Drain the pasta and return it to the pot.
  2. Add chopped chicken, condensed soup, diced tomatoes with chile peppers, processed cheese, mushrooms, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cook and stir over low heat until cheese is melted and the mixture is fully heated through. This should take about 5 minutes.
