Black Pepper Beef and Cabbage Stir Fry

Description

Quick, easy to make, and delicious black pepper beef with cabbage stir fry.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 small head cabbage, shredded
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into strips
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 1 pinch salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Heat a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat, and add oil. Saute garlic for about 5 seconds, then add ground beef. Stir-fry until beef is evenly bfown, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain excess fat.
  3. Stir in cabbage and pepper, and cook until vegetablesa re tender and beef is fully cooked. Stir in soy sauce.
  4. Mix together cornstarch and water, and stir in. Season with pepper, add salt to taste. Cook, stirring until sauce has thickened.
